Where to spend the night

  1. Free camping located right on the venue. There is a field in front of the Pearkes Event Centre where you can set up a tent or park an RV for the weekend. There are washrooms inside but no showers.

  2. Camping at King Gething's Campground just across the field. This one has showers and RV plugin. 4 minute walk to the venue. Online campground reservations can be made starting on May 3rd.

  3. Hotel at Stillwater Inn and Suites, located about 2 km from the venue. Please phone (250) 783-5400 to make a booking. Ask for the rooms booked under Darian for the July 30 weekend/wedding. Please make all bookings by May 30, 2022. 25-30 minute walk to venue; 5 minute drive.
